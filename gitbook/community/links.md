### Add $CLAW to Your Wallet

To add $CLAW to MetaMask or other wallets:

1. Open your wallet's token import/add token feature
2. Select "Custom Token" or "Import Token"
3. Enter the token contract address:
   ```
   0x4ba1209b165b62a1f0d2fbf22d67cacf43a6df2b
   ```
4. Symbol and decimals should auto-populate:
   - Symbol: `CLAW`
   - Decimals: `18`
5. Confirm the addition
